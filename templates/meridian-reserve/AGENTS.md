# Meridian Reserve Template Agent Guide

## Route
- `/templates/meridian-reserve`

## Best Fit
- Modern capital or acquisition firms
- Operators who want a sharper, more current brand feel
- Real estate or deal-oriented businesses that still need generalized wording

## Tone
- Decisive
- Premium and current
- Structured and fast-moving
- Confident without hype

## Personalize These First
- Replace `Meridian Reserve` with the client brand
- Update the hero headline with a broad description of the firm's offer
- Rewrite the hero paragraph using the intake summary
- Tailor the three services toward the client's real workflow
- Adjust process steps to match how the firm actually handles inquiries
- Add actual contact info, business address, and service area

## Guardrails
- Do not make regulated lending claims or guarantee capital outcomes
- Avoid hype language like `revolutionary`, `industry-leading`, or `instant approvals`
- Keep the site useful for validation first, marketing second
- Preserve broad phrases like `strategic`, `execution`, `opportunity`, `capital support`, `coordination`

## Design Notes
- This template can handle darker brand colors, copper accents, and higher contrast
- Preserve the cinematic feel and strong typography
- Avoid cluttering it with too many sections

## Required Footer/Legal
- Add the privacy policy or SMS disclosure if forms or texting are used
- Include correct contact details and any entity name suffix
- Add a basic rights-reserved footer
