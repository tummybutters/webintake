# Alder Crest Advisory Template Agent Guide

## Route
- `/templates/alder-crest`

## Best Fit
- Relationship-led capital businesses
- Boutique advisory firms
- Real estate groups that want a softer luxury look
- Clients who want premium without feeling too hard or corporate

## Tone
- Warm and polished
- Trustworthy and human
- High-end but approachable
- Broad and intentionally flexible

## Personalize These First
- Replace `Alder Crest Advisory` with the client name
- Rewrite the headline and intro using the intake description
- Update services to reflect the client's real work categories
- Align contact CTA labels with the preferred contact method
- Add the service area and business address
- Replace placeholder email and phone

## Guardrails
- Keep claims generalized unless the client has exact approved wording
- Avoid guarantee language like `fast approvals`, `best rates`, `guaranteed funding`, or similar
- Use elegant, simple wording over sales-heavy phrasing
- Keep copy adaptable for consulting, investing, capital introductions, or transaction support

## Design Notes
- This template works best with muted greens, warm neutrals, or soft gold accents
- If a logo is missing, use a refined text mark instead of a generic icon
- Keep spacing airy and premium

## Required Footer/Legal
- Insert full business name and address
- Add privacy/SMS language if the contact flow includes texting
- Include real contact details and copyright year
