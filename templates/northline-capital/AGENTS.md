# Northline Capital Template Agent Guide

## Route
- `/templates/northline-capital`

## Best Fit
- Fast capital firms
- Real estate operators
- Land or asset opportunity groups
- Advisory businesses that want a dark, premium, institutional feel

## Tone
- Quiet confidence
- Selective and credible
- Premium and restrained
- Broad enough for validation use

## Personalize These First
- Replace `Northline Capital` with the client business name
- Replace all placeholder contact info
- Update the hero headline to reflect the client's broad offer
- Rewrite the hero body from the intake's business description
- Map the three service cards to the client's real offer
- Update the process language to match the client's workflow
- Add the real service area
- Update CTA wording to the preferred contact method from intake

## Guardrails
- Keep the language broad and credible
- Do not promise funding, approvals, timelines, or outcomes unless the intake explicitly supports it
- Avoid regulated or compliance-sensitive claims unless the client specifically provides approved language
- Prefer terms like `advisory`, `opportunity`, `capital`, `coordination`, `evaluation`, `support`, `strategic`
- Keep the copy premium, concise, and bank-validation friendly

## Design Notes
- Preserve the dark luxury palette unless the intake includes brand colors
- If brand colors are supplied, map them into accents and button styling only
- Do not add loud gradients or startup-style language

## Required Footer/Legal
- Add the real business address
- Add the real email and phone
- Add a privacy policy block if the client uses forms, calls, or SMS
- Keep the footer simple and formal
